Denys Iarmak is a high-level member of the criminal hacking group FIN7. He was sentenced to five years in prison by a U.S. judge in April 2022. Iarmak initially declared himself not guilty in May 2020 but changed his plea in November. Iarmak, a Ukrainian national, was involved with the Russia-linked hacking group between November 2016 and November 2018.
